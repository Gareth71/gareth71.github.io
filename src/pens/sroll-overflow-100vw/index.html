Scrollbars cause overflow with 100vw

It is a bad idea to use width: 100vw; as it will cause horizontal scrolling as soon as your page is tall enough to have vertical scrolling.

Classic scrollbars are currently not taken into account with 100vw, but they are with width: 100%. Apple devices don't use classic scrollbars unless you turn them on at the system level.

System Preferences > General. Change "Show scroll bars:" to Always.

width: 100vw;
width: 100vw; max-width: 100%;
width: 100%;
